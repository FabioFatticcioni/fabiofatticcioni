About

Fabio is a concept artist and game artist based in Italy and currently works at Marshmallow Games making characters, environments, UI and animations for Smart Tales.
He has taught Spine as a professor, worked on several projects at various studios and created the mobile game Move Em’ All with a Game Designer friend.
Fabio’s passion is creating characters and worlds that communicate compelling stories and bring them to life.
